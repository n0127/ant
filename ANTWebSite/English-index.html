ABOUT ANT

ANT token is an ant token, based on the service of the real economy, empowering traditional industries, using the pass to connect merchants and users together to form a major business alliance, bid farewell to the era of single-handed fighting and fighting alone.ANT is committed to creating a safe, efficient and transparent certificate-based economic application system, which allows merchants and users to form a community of interests, and ultimately realizes that merchant products are not for sale. Users can purchase products with discounts and enjoy the mutual benefit of merchants. Win mode.ANT token community is an open, transparent, tokenized organization, and ANT Token represents the legal rights of the ANT token Business Alliance.ANT token adopts the ANT Token to enjoy the dividend equity model. The merchant distributes part of the proceeds to the ANT Token holder according to the proportion of ANT Token. The more ANT Token, the higher the equity. In addition, each ANT Token holder has the right to participate in community business decisions, team elections and other community activities.ANT token is a community-based organization that is shared, shared, and shared by all ANT Token holders and affiliate merchants.

ANT token

Platform features

  • Lightning Transfer Payment

    In order to solve the problems of cumbersome payment and slow transfer of digital assets, we build a new payment system based on multi-layer cyclic encryption technology, which provides one-stop payment solutions for all asset users in the system, and opens up a bridge between users and merchants for quick and free trading. It takes only 1 second to complete transfer payment.

  • Storing multiple digital assets

    It supports the storage of all digital currencies in the world, and accrues interest, so that it likes to deposit your digital currency in a bank, and it is safe, reliable and convenient for accruing interest.

  • Blockchain Mall

    Create a large-scale blockchain mall to help companies sell products at low cost, save inventory and channel costs; let users enjoy the purchase pleasure with low discounts, and enjoy dividends.Create a pass-through economy, let the world have no hard-to-sell products, and let everyone hold the pass to enjoy the mall dividends.

    VR is the abbreviation of Virtual Reality. Its meaning in Chinese is virtual reality. This concept was put forward in the early 1980s.
